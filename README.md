## Travel Tape

Travel Tape is a Travel Content Streaming platform, which helps planing your next travel.
It includes:-
- Road trips
- Group trips with your friends and families
- Solo travel 
- Adventure trips
- Budget Travel
- Luxury Travel

### Landing page
- Landing page will take us to the explore videos page.
- It has options to choose a specific category of videos
